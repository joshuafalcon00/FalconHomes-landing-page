Who we are

Falcon Homes is a house-and-lot builder focused on one community: Falcon Ridge. We design, build, and sell quality homes made for the way families really live, then hand over the keys faster than anyone expects.

We keep our scope tight on purpose. By building within a single, well-planned community instead of scattering across dozens of sites, we control quality, keep timelines short, and know every lot on the map by heart.

The Falcon Ridge community

Falcon Ridge is a modern, master-planned neighborhood organized into three phases. Every home sits on a numbered lot, so you're not just choosing a floor plan. You're choosing exactly where on the ridge your family will live.

Six distinct house styles share the same streets, landscaping, and standards. Whatever style fits you, you get the same dependable build and the same connected community.
